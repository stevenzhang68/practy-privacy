# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 15, 2025

Practy (“we”, “our”, or “the App”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it.  
This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and handled when you use the Practy application.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide
Practy does **not require account registration**.

We do **not collect personal information** such as your name, email address, phone number, or any other identifying details.

Videos and practice materials that you import into the app are **processed and stored locally on your device only**.  
We do **not upload, store, or analyze your videos or media content on our servers**.

---

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information
To improve app performance and user experience, Practy collects limited, **non-personal and anonymous information**, including:

- Anonymous usage data (such as feature usage and interaction events)
- Device information (for example, device model and operating system version)
- App performance metrics
- Crash reports and diagnostic data

This information is collected in a way that **does not directly identify you**.

---

## 2. How We Use Information

Collected information is used solely to:

- Improve app functionality and usability
- Analyze feature usage and performance
- Identify and fix bugs or crashes
- Ensure reliable in-app purchase and subscription functionality

Practy does **not** use collected data for advertising, profiling, or marketing purposes.

---

## 3. Third-Party Services

Practy uses the following third-party services to operate and improve the app:

### Analytics
- **PostHog**  
  Used to collect anonymous usage analytics to understand how features are used and to improve the app experience.

### In-App Purchases
- **RevenueCat**  
  Used to manage App Store subscriptions and purchase status.  
  Practy does not collect or store payment information. All payments are processed securely by Apple.

### Crash Reporting
- **Bugly**  
  Used to collect crash reports and diagnostic data to improve app stability and performance.

These third-party services process data in accordance with their own privacy policies and only for the purposes described above.

---

## 4. Location Information

Practy may access **approximate or precise location information** as provided by the operating system.  
Location data is used only for:

- App functionality
- Usage analysis

Location information is **not used for tracking or advertising** and is not stored in a way that identifies individual users.

---

## 5. Data Sharing

Practy does **not sell, rent, or share** personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

Data is shared only with the third-party service providers listed above, strictly as necessary to operate and improve the app.

---

## 6. Data Retention

- Anonymous usage analytics and diagnostic data are retained only as long as necessary for analysis and improvement.
- All videos and practice materials you import into the app remain on your device and are not retained by Practy.

---

## 7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, delete, or restrict the processing of your data.

Because Practy does not maintain user accounts or store personal identifying information, most collected data is anonymous and cannot be linked back to an individual user.

If you have questions about data usage, you may contact us using the information below.

---

## 8. Children’s Privacy

Practy does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

The app does not require account creation and does not request personal identifying information.

---

## 9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.  
Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

---

## 10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

**Email:** practy@163.com

---

# 隐私政策

**最后更新日期：** 2025年3月15日

Practy（以下简称“我们”或“本应用”）非常重视您的隐私保护。本隐私政策说明了您在使用 Practy 应用过程中，相关信息的收集、使用与处理方式。

---

## 1. 我们收集的信息

### 1.1 您主动提供的信息
Practy **不需要用户注册账号**。

我们 **不会收集** 您的姓名、电子邮箱、电话号码或任何可识别您身份的个人信息。

您导入到应用中的练习视频和素材 **仅在您的设备本地进行处理和存储**。  
我们 **不会将您的视频或媒体内容上传、存储或分析到我们的服务器**。

---

### 1.2 自动收集的信息
为了改进应用性能和用户体验，Practy 会收集有限的、**非个人且匿名的信息**，包括：

- 匿名的使用数据（例如功能使用情况、交互事件）
- 设备信息（如设备型号、操作系统版本）
- 应用性能数据
- 崩溃日志及诊断数据

这些信息 **不会直接识别您的个人身份**。

---

## 2. 信息的使用方式

我们仅将收集的信息用于：

- 改进应用功能和使用体验
- 分析功能使用情况和性能表现
- 排查和修复程序错误或崩溃问题
- 确保应用内购买和订阅功能的正常运行

我们 **不会** 将这些信息用于广告、用户画像或营销目的。

---

## 3. 第三方服务

Practy 使用以下第三方服务来支持应用运行和改进体验：

### 数据分析
- **PostHog**  
  用于收集匿名的使用分析数据，以了解功能使用情况并优化应用体验。

### 应用内购买
- **RevenueCat**  
  用于管理 App Store 的订阅和购买状态。  
  Practy 不会收集或存储任何支付信息，所有支付均由 Apple 安全处理。

### 崩溃收集
- **Bugly**  
  用于收集应用崩溃和诊断信息，以提升应用稳定性。

上述第三方服务仅会在必要范围内处理相关数据，并遵循其各自的隐私政策。

---

## 4. 位置信息

Practy 可能会访问由操作系统提供的 **粗略或精确位置信息**，仅用于：

- 应用功能支持
- 使用情况分析

位置信息 **不会用于用户追踪或广告用途**，也不会以可识别个人身份的方式进行存储。

---

## 5. 数据共享

Practy **不会出售、出租或以营销目的共享**您的个人数据。

数据仅在应用运行和改进所必需的范围内，与上述第三方服务提供方共享。

---

## 6. 数据保留

- 匿名的使用分析数据和诊断数据仅在必要期限内保留
- 您导入的所有视频和练习素材始终保留在您的设备本地，不会由 Practy 保存

---

## 7. 您的权利

根据您所在地区的法律，您可能拥有访问、删除或限制数据处理的相关权利。

由于 Practy 不维护用户账号，也不存储可识别个人身份的信息，大多数收集的数据均为匿名数据，无法与具体个人关联。

如有任何关于数据使用的疑问，您可以通过下方联系方式与我们联系。

---

## 8. 儿童隐私

Practy 不会在明知的情况下收集儿童的个人信息。

应用不要求注册账号，也不会主动请求任何个人身份信息。

任何变更都会在本页面公布，并更新相应的日期。
